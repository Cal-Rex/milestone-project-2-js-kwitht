How to Do it! and not Die!

1. Do the time and don't get beat by the crimes

  • When you start the game you will begin a random encounter
  • You gotta either Do it! or Run Away!
  • If you Do it! and win, you will progress on your Journey
  • If you Run Away! well, you ran, so you get nothing, loser!
  • You can fail if you try to Do it! or Run Away!. If you fail, You Die.

2. I had a dentist appointment on leg day, what did i miss?

  • QWETHT encounters are determined by random dice rolls behind the scenes (like that guy in the Wizard of Oz!)
  • In QWETHT the encounters roll aginst you! (like D&D, but in reverse, you are the goblin now!)
  • The Strength stat determines how good you are at strength stuff (jumping, fighting, bein strong)
  • The Cunning stat determines how much smarts you got, (how to trick people, or be savvy, or read books dead good)
  • The Agility stat determines how good you are at running away. (wimp!)

3. Planning your End-of-Life care with AXA Sunlife

  • Your QWETHT will get harder every time you beat an encounter
  • Between 0% - 40% is easy. Encounters can't roll higher than 4 (suckers)
  • Between 50% - 70% is Lemon & herb. Encounters can't roll higher than 6 (like 6 6-year-olds in a trenchcoat)
  • Between 80% - 100% is Dance Dance Revolution. Encounters can roll up to 9 (They don't wash their hands before dinner!)

Qwetht

Welcome to Qwetht. It's a game. The aim of the game is to complete the Qwetht by doing stuff. Every Qwetht is different. Encounters are random, you are random, the reason for being on your qwetht is random.
Qwethts can be pretty unforgiving, You can take action or run away from an encounter, but death can loom around the corner of any choice you make. Take care in your choices and good luck!
